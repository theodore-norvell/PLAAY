Built in functions for PLAAY

+ Add 1 or more numbers.
- Subtract 1 or more numbers.
* Multiply 1 or more numbers.
/ Divide 1 or more numbers.
> Greater than.
< Less than.
>= Greater than or equal.
<= Less than or equal.
= Equality
and Conjunction.
or Disjunction.

Turtle graphics commands

forward n Move forward n steps.
right d Turn right by d degrees.
left d Turn left by d degrees.
penUp Stop drawing.
penDown Start drawing.
hide Hide the turtle.
show Show the turtle.
clear Clear the screen and reset the turtle.
